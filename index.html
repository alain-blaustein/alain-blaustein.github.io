Presentation

Welcome to my webpage. I am interested in the theoretical and numerical analysis of mean-field models arising in Biology and Physics.

I am a research scientist (Chargé de Recherche de Classe Normale) in the Inria team RAPSODI.

I held a S. Chowla Research Assistant position at the Department of Mathematics of the Pennsylvania State University from August 2023 to December 2024.

My Ph.D. was defended in June 2023 and supervised by Francis Filbet at the Institut de Mathématiques de Toulouse.

This webpage gathers information and documents about my research, my teaching activities and my education.
